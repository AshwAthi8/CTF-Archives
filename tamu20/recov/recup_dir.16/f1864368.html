    I need to access MSN Explorer through a proxy

    1. Click the Start button, point to Settings, and then click Control Panel.

    2. Double-click the Internet Options icon.


    3. Click the Connections tab.


    4. Click LAN settings, and make sure that the Use a proxy server check box is selected. Enter the correct address and port settings, per your LAN administrator.


    5. Click OK.


    6. Under Dial-up settings, make sure Never dial a connection is selected.


    7. Click OK.


    8. Sign in to MSN Explorer.
