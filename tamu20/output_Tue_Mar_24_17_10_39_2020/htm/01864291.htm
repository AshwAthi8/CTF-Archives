Why we need to know your birthday

We need to know your age if you are subscribing to MSN Internet Access because members of this service must be at least 18 years old.

Also, U.S. federal law requires parental consent for the collection or posting on the Internet of personal information from children under the age of 13. We can only assign the correct type of account (adult account or child account) when we know the dates of birth for you and other users in your household.
