To view your default connection settings:

If you're using: Do this:
Windows 95 or 98 On the desktop, double-click My Computer. Double-click Dial-Up Networking.
Windows Millennium Click Start, point to Settings, then Control Panel. Double-click Dial-Up Networking.
Windows 2000 and above Click Start, point to Settings, then Control Panel. Double-click Network and Dial-up Connections.
