To view or change your default connection settings:

  1. Click Start, point to Settings, and then Control Panel.

  2. Double-click Internet Options.

  3. Click Connections.

  4. If you're using a dial-up connection, in the Dial-up settings list, select the connection you want to make your default, and then click Set Default.

    –or–

    If you're using a broadband connection, click LAN Settings and verify that the settings are correct.

  5. Click OK.
