MSN Broadband minimum requirements

  • Microsoft Windows 98, Windows 98 Second Edition (SE), Windows ME, Windows 2000 or Windows XP (Microsoft Windows 95 is not supported)
  • 166 MHz Pentium or equivalent processor
  • 16 MB of RAM (32 MB or higher recommended)
  • Microsoft Mouse or compatible pointing device recommended
  • VGA or higher resolution graphics card
  • Minimum 800x600 resolution recommended
  • Sound card and speakers recommended
  • Available USB or Ethernet port on the main computer (not on a hub or serial connected through other devices)
  • CD-ROM drive
